Dr.Stethowire App

Description:

Dr.stethowire is an innovative mobile app that connects to an Arduino-based stethoscope via Bluetooth. It captures audio signals, processes them, and generates detailed medical reports.

Key Features:

  • Bluetooth Connectivity: Seamlessly connects to the Arduino stethoscope.
  • Signal Processing: Analyzes audio data for heartbeat rates, lung sounds, and other medical information.
  • PDF Report Generation: Creates comprehensive reports with visualizations.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Intuitive design for recording, saving, and sharing reports.

Potential Use Cases:

  • Telemedicine and Remote Monitoring: Enables remote patient monitoring and consultations.
  • Medical Education and Training: Useful for students and practitioners to practice auscultation.
  • Personal Health Tracking: Individuals can monitor their own health using the app.
  • Clinical Research and Studies: Contributes to research on heart and lung health.
